Welcome to the place where healing happens with Allah at the center — because real change, real peace, and real becoming can only come from Him.

This is for you if...

You know there's more. You're just not sure how to get there.

  • ✦ You've been carrying a quiet heaviness — and you're ready to put it down.
  • ✦ You want to heal but you don't know where to begin.
  • ✦ You feel like everyone is okay except you — and you're exhausted pretending.
  • ✦ You want a life rooted in tawakkul, not just motivation that fades.
  • ✦ You're a Muslimah navigating life, love, and becoming — and you need a reminder that Allah is enough.
  • ✦ You want to stop drifting and start living with intention and faith.

Here's what I know to be true.

Healing cannot happen without Allah. People come and go. Seasons change. Life rearranges itself in ways we never planned for. But Allah is the only constant — and when you make Him the center of everything, everything else begins to fall into place.

I learned this not from a book, but from living it. And now I write so you remember it too.

"And He found you lost and guided you." — Surah Ad-Duha, 93:7

Hi, I'm Nabila.

Before with love, nabila had a name — before a single word was published — I discovered something that changed everything: the peace of making Allah the absolute center of my life.

I started writing letters. Not for an audience, but for myself — from healing, through the hard seasons and the quiet ones. And then I realized: the whole world is healing. Someone may need to read this.

So I started publishing on Substack. And now I want to go bigger — because this message is too important to stay small.

I write for Muslimahs navigating life. The beautiful parts, the broken parts, and everything in between. I write about dua, tawakkul, the miracles of Allah, and what it means to be truly seen by Him.
