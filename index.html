Welcome to the place where healing happens with Allah at the center — because real change, real peace, and real becoming can only come from Him.

This is for you if...

You know there is more.
You are just not sure how to get there.

  • ✦You have been carrying a quiet heaviness — and you are ready to put it down.
  • ✦You want to heal but you do not know where to begin.
  • ✦You feel like everyone is okay except you — and you are exhausted pretending.
  • ✦You want a life rooted in tawakkul, not just motivation that fades.
  • ✦You are a Muslimah navigating life, love, and becoming — and you need a reminder that Allah is enough.
  • ✦You want to stop drifting and start living with intention and faith.

Here is what I know to be true.

Healing cannot happen without Allah. People come and go. Seasons change. Life rearranges itself in ways we never planned for. But Allah is the only constant — and when you make Him the center of everything, everything else begins to fall into place.

I learned this not from a book, but from living it. And now I write so you remember it too.

"And He found you lost and guided you." — Surah Ad-Duha · 93:7

Hi, I'm Nabila.

Before With Love, Nabila had a name — before a single word was published — I found something that changed everything: the peace of making Allah the absolute center of my life.

I started writing letters. Not for an audience, but for myself — from healing, through the hard seasons and the quiet ones. Then I realized: the whole world is healing. Someone may need to read this.

So I started publishing. And now I want to go bigger — because this message is too important to stay small.
