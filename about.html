The story behind the brand

About With Love,
Nabila

A brand built from healing, rooted in faith, and written with love.

"And He found you lost and guided you." This is the ayah that started everything. The reminder that where I am now is not where I have to stay. — Surah Ad-Duha · 93:7

How With Love, Nabila began

This started as a letter from healing — written to myself, in the quiet moments between dua and dawn. Words that didn't need an audience. Just a girl, her journal, and Allah.

And then something shifted. I realized: the whole world is healing. Someone, somewhere, needs to read this. So I started publishing on Substack. And now, alhamdulillah, I want to go bigger — because this message is too important to stay small.

Before the writing, there was Allah

Before With Love, Nabila had a name — before a single word was published — I found something that changed everything: the peace of making Allah the center of every single thing in my life.

I learned that real healing cannot happen without Allah. That He is the only true constant — people come and go, seasons change, and life never stays the same. But Allah? Allah is always there. He was there when no one else was. He saw me when I felt invisible.

And in that knowing, I fell deeply in love with dua and tawakkul. With the miracle of being heard. With the beauty of trusting fully — not in outcomes, not in people, but in Al-Wakeel, the One who handles all affairs.

What this space is for

This space is for Muslimahs navigating life — the beautiful parts, the broken parts, and everything in between. You will find essays on healing, faith, tawakkul, and becoming. You will find honesty, softness, and always — always — a reminder that Allah is enough.
